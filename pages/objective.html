Objective Testing

Objective Tests are multiple-choice exams that assess a student's knowledge on various business-related subjects. Each test consists of 50-100 multiple-choice questions and is timed (~60 minutes).

What To Know

  • If you choose to only compete in objective tests, you do not physically attend Pre-Area. Some Role-Play or Presentational Events require an objective test.
