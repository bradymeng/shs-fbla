Pre Area

Pre Area is a Stevenson only competitition stage held within Stevenson High School and judged by Stevenson business teachers. This stage a the prerequisite to being eligible to compete at the Nothern Area Conferance held later in January.

Important Information!

  • Date/Time: October 24, 2024; 4:00-7:00pm
  • Location: 2400s Hallway, Stevenson High School
  • Competitors must wear Official FBLA Dress Code
  • Check in 15 minutes prior to event. Then find your team's room.

Prompt 1:

  • 24-25 prompt 1: Find a company struggling due to current events, define the struggle , and propose a solution.
  • Rubric:
  • - Provide Business Overview and Summary
  • - Demonstrate Awareness of Current Events Impacting the Business
  • - Identify a Relevent and Recent Corporate Struggle and Causes
  • - Propose Innovative Solution to Problem
  • - Use Relevant, Reliable Sources that are Cleary Cited
  • - Demonstrate Self-Confidence, Poise, Assertiveness
  • - Follow FBLA Dress Code

Prompt 2:

  • 24-25 prompt 2: Compare 2 companies in the same industry, highlight similarities/differences, and propose the more successful company.
  • Rubric:
  • - Give Detailed Overview of Both Companies
  • - Effectively Descirbe Similarities
  • - Describe the Differences in Business Conduct, Structure, Operations, and Strategies
  • - Effectively Conclude Which Company was More Successful
  • - Use Relevant, Reliable Sources that are Cleary Cited
  • - Demonstrate Self-Confidence, Poise, Assertiveness
  • - Follow FBLA Dress Code

Presentation Details

  • - 5 Minute Presentation Time. Points deducted if over time
  • - 2 Minute Questioning by Judges
  • - Groups Consist of 3 Competitors
  • There Will Be Cuts!
